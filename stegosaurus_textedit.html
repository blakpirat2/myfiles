she don’t wanna really talk--she wanna plant seeds

that’ll sprout up into doubts ‘bout who i really can be

add in water from my martyr complex canteen

with a high molarity of my own anxieties 


my


leaves seem healthy but feel fear in their DNA

an adaptive remnant of the past on display

a miraculous invention that had its own place

on the mantle photosynthesizing hazardous waste 


this


game of chicken? stupid bird to play. 

how about a game of stegosaurus? ride the vertebrae plates.

how about a game of polar bear--being cuddly and wrathful?

how about a game of baboon--chucking shit from an asshole? 


we


wanna be reborn but endangered the bees so

my future is pollinated with cancerous seeds

mixed messages conflating chrysanthemums and the weeds

simultaneously laying anathema on the peat.
